Eligibility & Documents

Age Requirements (as of June 1, 2025)

  • • Pre-Nursery: 2.5 - 3.5 years
  • • Nursery: 3.5 - 4.5 years
  • • LKG: 4.5 - 5.5 years
  • • UKG: 5.5 - 6.5 years
  • • Grade 1: 6.5+ years

Academic Prerequisites

  • • For Pre-Nursery to UKG: No academic prerequisites
  • • For Grades 1-8: Successful completion of previous grade
  • • For Grades 9-12: Minimum 60% in previous grade

Entrance Assessment

  • • Pre-Nursery to Grade 1: Informal interaction
  • • Grades 2-8: Basic assessment in English, Math, and General Knowledge
  • • Grades 9-12: Subject-specific assessments

Student Documents

  • • Birth Certificate (original + 2 photocopies)
  • • Passport-sized photographs (4 copies)
  • • Immunization record
  • • Previous school report cards (for Grade 1 and above)
  • • Transfer Certificate (for Grade 1 and above)

Parent/Guardian Documents

  • • ID proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport)
  • • Address proof (not older than 3 months)
  • • Passport-sized photographs (2 copies each)

Additional Documents (if applicable)

  • • Caste certificate (for reservation category)
  • • Disability certificate (if applicable)
  • • Guardian authorization (if parents are not the guardians)

Frequently Asked Questions

The admission process involves submitting an application form along with required documents, followed by document verification. For grades 2 and above, students need to take an entrance assessment. The final step is an interview and confirmation of admission upon selection.

Vishwa Bharathi School follows the CBSE curriculum with a holistic approach to education. We emphasize academic excellence along with co-curricular activities, sports, and value education to ensure the all-round development of students.

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships ranging from 10% to 50% of the tuition fee. Scholarships are awarded based on academic excellence, sports achievements, and cultural talents. Students can apply for scholarships after admission confirmation.

The school timings are as follows:

  • • Pre-Nursery to KG: 8:30 AM to 12:30 PM
  • • Grades 1 to 5: 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM
  • • Grades 6 to 12: 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM

Yes, the school provides transportation facilities for students. Our buses cover most areas in the city. The transportation fee varies based on the distance from school. All buses are equipped with GPS tracking and are monitored for safety.
