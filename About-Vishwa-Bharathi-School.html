Our Story

Founded in 1985 by visionary educator Dr. Rajendra Kumar, Vishwa Bharathi School began with a modest campus and 120 students. Today, we stand as one of the region's premier educational institutions with state-of-the-art facilities and a legacy of academic excellence spanning four decades.

Our name "Vishwa Bharathi" reflects our philosophy of global education rooted in Indian values, preparing students to be responsible global citizens while maintaining a strong cultural identity.

Our Journey

1985

Founded by Dr. Rajendra Kumar with 120 students and 15 faculty members in a small rented building.

1992

Moved to our current 5-acre campus with expanded facilities and introduced secondary education.

2001

Received CBSE affiliation and launched our first computer lab and digital library.

2010

Celebrated 25 years of excellence and inaugurated our state-of-the-art sports complex.

2022

Launched STEM Innovation Center and received National School of Excellence Award.

Educational Philosophy

Holistic Development

We believe education goes beyond textbooks. Our curriculum balances academics, sports, arts, and character development to nurture well-rounded individuals.

Inquiry-Based Learning

We encourage curiosity and critical thinking through hands-on experiences, project-based learning, and real-world applications of knowledge.

Global Perspective

Our curriculum integrates international best practices while celebrating Indian cultural heritage, preparing students for global citizenship.
